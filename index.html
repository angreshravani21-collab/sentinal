02 // SECURITY MODULES

Flip the Clause

Hover any module to decrypt its control description. Straight from ISO/IEC 27002 Annex A.

CLAUSE 5.16
🪪

Identity Lifecycle

Every human, bot, and service gets one unique identity, traceable end to end. Shared accounts need documented approval. Leavers get disabled fast — accountability isn't optional.
CLAUSE 5.17
🔑

Authentication Info

Temporary passwords are random, unique, one-time-use. Default vendor credentials get changed on day one. Nothing travels over plain email — ever.
CLAUSE 5.18
🗝️

Access Rights

Access follows least privilege and needs asset-owner sign-off. Approver and implementer are never the same person — segregation of duties, enforced.
CLAUSE 5.19
🤝

Supplier Security

Suppliers are evaluated, scoped, and monitored — every risky component of their access to your systems gets mapped before the contract is signed.
CLAUSE 5.20
📜

Supplier Agreements

Contracts spell out classification, incident response, audit rights, and secure teardown — so when the relationship ends, so does every byte of access.
